Teachings From Liz Lemon: How To Have It All!

Do any of these apply to you?

  • Are you a work-a-holic who wants to succeed in all facets of life?
  • Do other people constantly rely on you and badger you to fix their problems?
  • Do you ever just want to quit all of your responsibilities and just eat a sandwich?
Then Lizbeanism might be for you!


Now what will Lizbeanism mean for you?

Lizbeanism is all about following the teachings of the illustrious Liz Lemon: Head-writer for TGS w/ Tracy Jordan, Mentee of the esteemed Jack Donaghy , Author of the New York Times Best Seller Deal Breakers and all around kick-butt lady. She has had many problems thrown her way and she powers through them all with aplomb, a little bit of scotch tape and the power of Lizbeanism. By following her example you will be able to tackle any and all issues in life head-on.
